OpenMatch is a job board I built for a talent-graph company: a full two-sided platform connecting candidates and recruiters, with AI woven into the resume and matching experience.

## What I built

- **For candidates:** AI-assisted resume building (including quiz-based creation), ATS-friendly templates, resume scoring, and job matching.
- **For recruiters:** job posting, candidate matching and recruiter tooling.
- **Platform:** subscription billing with Stripe, dashboards and charts with ApexCharts, and a rich UI built on Nuxt and Vuetify.

## The stack

Nuxt and Vue.js with Vuex state management, Vuetify for the component system, Stripe for payments and ApexCharts for analytics dashboards.
